Are Tumeze supplements suitable for children?

Live cultures are natural food supplements and not medicines, so customers should be able to find live cultures which are completely safe for infants and children. We do however, always recommend asking your family GP before putting children under 5 years onto any new kind of supplement.

Does it take time to adjust after I switch probiotics?

While it can take your body up to two weeks in order to adjust to Tumeze, most people are able to make the switch instantly and successfully!

How many billions is best in a live culture supplement?

AThere are lots of friendly bacteria products on the market, many of which boast very high billion counts. Whilst this is not harmful, it is not necessarily any better than a supplement with a lower CFU. It's something of a myth that a super-high billions count is superior.

It's much more important to consider the quality of live cultures - for example, whether the strains being used have been widely tested for safety and are able to reach the gut alive. Furthermore, different strains of friendly bacteria have different modes of action in the body, so it's important to take the right live cultures for you, rather than focus too much on the number of billions.

What does it mean to subscribe to Tumeze?

A subscription to Tumeze allows you to receive one or multiple Tumeze products at a consistent frequency. This way, you never run out of Tumeze! Tumeze probiotic is £12 per probiotic if you purchase individually, or £10 per probiotic if you subscribe.

Please note that certain items are not available through a subscription.

Once you have subscribed, you will be in complete control of your subscription. You can change the scents you receive, the frequency you receive them or even your next shipping date at any time. In addition, you can update your address, cancel your subscription, or skip a shipment at any time simply by logging into your account.

Can I take Tumeze if I have Coeliac Disease or Wheat Intolerance?

All Tumeze supplements are gluten free and wheat free. They are therefore suitable for those suffering from Coeliac Disease, a gluten intolerance or wheat intolerance.

I have an allergy. How do I know if your products are suitable for me?

We disclose information about the most common allergens on each product description page. Most of our products have no added Gluten, Soy and/or Lactose. However, due to the manufacturing process, we can't guarantee that our products are completely free of these allergens. If you have any concerns or if you have any other allergy, please contact our lovely team

Do Tumeze supplements require refrigeration?

his is one of the most frequent questions we are asked: should I put my live cultures in the fridge? Is it better to keep them in a refrigerator? The simple answer is no - there is absolutely no need to refrigerate any of the supplements in the Tumeze range.
